Can you explain Newton's Second Law with an example?
✦
Newton's Second Law states that F = ma — Force equals mass times acceleration.

Imagine pushing a shopping cart. A heavy cart requires more force to reach the same speed as a lighter one. The more mass, the more force needed to accelerate it!
